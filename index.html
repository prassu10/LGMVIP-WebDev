about us

why choose us?

best shakes in the city

frappé strives to source local, sustainable and organic when possible. We work hard to source premium ingredients and we cook everything from scratch with love. We also do our best to pay our employees living wages (tips are shared with all employees, including kitchen staff) and to reduce our environmental footprint wherever we can. Overall, these factors translate to higher menu prices, but we hope that you find value and feel a sense of comfort in knowing that we aim to get better everyday at doing what is important to us.

We have been around for 20 years, and we have been through some real ups and downs. But through it all we have served awesome pizza and chicken, that’s why we are sort of famous. Thanks for choosing us, we hope you enjoy.
